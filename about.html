Who we are

A small team with a trader's eye and an engineer's discipline

Santro AI is inspired by the AI boom — a wave that creates real opportunity and real edge, yet there was no simple tool to track it. So we decided to build one. It started as a personal trading terminal: one screen to see the whole AI sector breathing — what's running, what's bleeding, and what the story is. It's built on experience trading across asset classes — stocks, ETFs, US and international markets — with one goal: connect it all in one place.

Hot means attention, not direction. A stock down 28% on a $7B dilution is the day's biggest story — a feed that only shows winners is lying to you.
