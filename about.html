How Santro gets better

Trader-built. Market-updated.

Santro is built from the way traders actually work: watching the tape, noticing recurring questions, testing what matters, and turning useful workflows into product features. When a signal, tracker or tool becomes useful to our own process, we build it into the terminal. The product follows the market — and the market changes, so Santro changes with it.

Signal philosophy

Hot means attention, not direction.

A hot ticker is not automatically a buy. Heat can mean momentum, crowding, risk, overvaluation, narrative rotation or stress. Santro treats heat as a reason to investigate — not as a trading signal.
