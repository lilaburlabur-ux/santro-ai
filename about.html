About Santro AI Beta

Santro AI maps the AI side of the markets so you can read it in seconds.

What this is

A living bubble map of the AI stock universe — 83 names across seven sectors — where bubble size follows the day's move and color follows direction. Around the map: a daily research journal with signed per-stock reports, key takeaways refreshed through the trading day, a hot-tickers attention scan, AI-related ETF coverage, and a deduplicated news feed tagged by ticker and sector. Crypto coverage is being connected.

How it works

Market data is free, delayed (~15 minutes) and refreshed automatically on trading days. Research follows a momentum-first methodology: structure, levels and conditions — never buy/sell instructions. When something moves on no news, the site says exactly that.

What this is not

Not investment advice, not a broker, not real-time data. Santro AI is an educational research journal. Markets move fast; always do your own research.
