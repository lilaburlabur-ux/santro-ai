Read this first — it's the whole game

There are really only ~15 distinct companies inside this entire list (Nvidia, Broadcom, AMD, TSMC, Micron, Samsung, SK Hynix, Alphabet, Microsoft, Meta, Tesla, Palantir…). Every fund below is a different recipe using the same pantry. Don't let 40 tickers fool you into thinking there are 40 ideas.

So your real decisions are only three:

1 · How concentrated?2 · How much you pay?3 · US-only or global?

Proof — tap any fund for its weighted holdings: the same six names (Nvidia, AMD, Micron, Broadcom, TSMC, SK Hynix) are the engine of almost every “different” fund. What changes is how concentrated and how foreign — SMH packs 71% into its top 10 (Nvidia alone 14.5%), while KOID spreads to just 27% and goes heavily Asian. That spread is the real menu.
