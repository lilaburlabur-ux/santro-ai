Evaluate Your AI Trading Prompt

A good trading prompt is the difference between research and a fortune cookie. Paste yours below and the scorer grades it across nine dimensions — and tells you, bluntly, what is missing.

It checks direction, context, output format, examples, verification, live-data honesty, risk framing, hallucination risk and overall trading usefulness. The single most important check: whether your prompt asks the model for current prices or news it cannot actually see — the fastest way to get confident, fabricated market analysis. The scorer runs entirely in your browser; nothing is uploaded or stored, and it never rewrites your prompt — it shows you the gaps so you can fix them yourself.
