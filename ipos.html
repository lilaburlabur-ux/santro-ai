How the AI IPO tracker works

The AI listings story end to end, split into two lists:

  • Recently listed — AI names that have IPO'd, with their debut-day, first-week and first-month returns plus performance since the offer. Returns are anchored to each name's curated IPO date and offer price (you cannot infer those from a price chart), so the debut math is correct even for older listings.
  • Pre-IPO watchlist — the private AI names the market is waiting on (Anthropic, OpenAI, xAI, Databricks, Anduril and more), with what they do, last-known valuation and status. They have no ticker yet — there is no live price to show until they list.

Listed names link through to a full ticker page with a TradingView chart; pre-IPO names get a profile page. Quotes are ~15-minute delayed and pre-IPO valuations are last-known estimates.
