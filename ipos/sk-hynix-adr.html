SK hynix US listing — AI IPO watch

The HBM leader listing ADSs on Nasdaq (reported ticker SKHY)

What it is

SK hynix — the world's leading high-bandwidth-memory maker and Nvidia's primary HBM supplier — has filed a Form F-1 (foreign private issuer registration, CIK 0002120882, amendments through July 6, 2026) to list American Depositary Shares on Nasdaq while its primary listing stays in Seoul. This is not price discovery: the stock already trades on the KRX. It is the AI-memory leader raising US capital for the build-out.

The deal, as reported

Reported from the filing: ~177.9M ADSs (each 1/10th of a Korean share), net proceeds around $28B earmarked for capex, with the raise capped near 2.5% of the company because Korean holding-company law requires SK Square to keep at least a 20% stake. Reported non-binding cornerstone interest includes Baillie Gifford, Coatue and Situational Awareness Partners — the fund tracked on Santro's Aschenbrenner page. Treat all terms as subject to amendment until pricing.
