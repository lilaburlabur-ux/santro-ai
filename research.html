The research method

Every trading day, after the US close, Santro AI generates a signed research report for each tracked name — a repeatable decision-journal, not a hot take. Every report follows the same nine-section structure:

  • Executive summary, scored on momentum, setup quality, fundamentals, value and risk.
  • Company & recent catalysts, then technicals (52-week range, SMA 20/50/200, RSI, ATR, beta).
  • Fundamentals (revenue, margins, valuation, growth), institutional ownership, and a risk review with a fixed setup checklist.
  • A full data snapshot, recent analyst actions, and a conclusion with a sources table.

How to read it

It is momentum-first: the method asks whether a name has a defined setup — a tight base, a controlled pullback, or a confirmed reclaim of a key level — with risk sized in advance, never "chase the extended move." Metrics are computed from free, ~15-minute-delayed Yahoo Finance data and labelled as such, and reports refresh after each close so the archive always reflects the official last close.
