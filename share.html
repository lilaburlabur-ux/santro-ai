What the share cards are

Finished, branded market cards — sized to screenshot or post straight to X or Telegram, no editing needed. Each card is built from the same delayed data the rest of the site runs on, with the delay note and "not financial advice" baked in so you can share responsibly.

Card types

  • AI movers — the day's biggest moves across the AI universe, as a bubble snapshot.
  • Hot tickers — the attention leaders, with the sourced reason each is moving.
  • Top winners & losers — the clean leaderboard, both sides of the tape.
  • AI bubble risk — the current 0–100 score and its band, for the "how frothy is it" post.
  • AI crypto movers — the same treatment for the top AI tokens.

"Hot" means attention, not direction — a mover card shows what is moving and how hard, not what to buy.
