How the AI stocks bubble map works

The AI Universe view tracks roughly 83 AI-exposed companies drawn from the Finviz artificial-intelligence theme, grouped into seven sectors across the AI stack — from chips and compute to AI software, cloud and data-center, and applied AI. Toggle to the NVIDIA Ecosystem view for the 30 suppliers, customers and partners that orbit Nvidia specifically: memory, networking, foundry, testing, power and cooling.

Reading the bubbles

  • Size = how far the name moved today (absolute day move) — a bigger bubble is a bigger move, up or down.
  • Colour = direction, on a roughly ±10% green-to-red scale.
  • Click a bubble for price, the day's move, sector and a fair-value read, then open its full ticker page.

"Hot" means attention, not direction. A name lighting up the map is moving hard — a reason to look, not a signal to buy or sell. A 20% drop is exactly as "hot" as a 20% rip.

Data & limits

Quotes are free, ~15-minute-delayed Yahoo Finance data, refreshed automatically through the session and extended hours. Market cap is split-adjusted and corrupt vendor bars are repaired before display. This is market context for research, not a recommendation.
