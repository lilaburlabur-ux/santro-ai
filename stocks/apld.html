APLD — Applied Digital Corporation

Technology · Information Technology Services

About Applied Digital Corporation

Applied Digital Corporation designs, develops, and operates digital infrastructure solutions to high-performance computing (HPC) and artificial intelligence industries in North America. It operates through: Data Center Hosting Business, and HPC Hosting Business. The company offers infrastructure services to crypto mining customers; and GPU computing solutions for critical workloads related to AI, machine learning, and other HPC tasks. It also engages in the designing, constructing, and managing of data centers to support HPC applications.

The AI data-center infrastructure industry

AI data-center infrastructure firms provide the buildings, power and hosting that AI compute actually runs in — leasing high-density capacity, and often power and cooling, to hyperscalers and AI companies. Several began as crypto-mining operators and pivoted to AI and high-performance-computing hosting. The business turns on access to cheap power, land and capital, and on signing long-term contracts with creditworthy tenants; it is capital-hungry and sensitive to financing costs and to how durable AI demand proves to be. Applied Digital builds and operates data centers and offers cloud and high-performance-computing hosting aimed at AI workloads.
