INOD — Innodata Inc.

Technology · Information Technology Services

About Innodata Inc.

Innodata Inc. operates as a data engineering company in the United States, the United Kingdom, the Netherlands, Canada, Germany, Belgium, and internationally. The company operates through three segments: Digital Data Solutions (DDS), Synodex, and Agility. The DDS segment engages in the provision of artificial intelligence (AI) training and post-training data, model evaluation, alignment, safety, AI model deployment and integration, and AI-enabled platforms.

The AI data-services industry

The AI data-services industry provides the training data, annotation, curation and evaluation that machine-learning models depend on — the human-and-software pipeline that turns raw data into model-ready datasets. Demand is tied directly to how much AI development is happening, especially large-model training and fine-tuning, though the field faces open questions about pricing power and how much of the work automation will eventually absorb. Innodata provides these data-engineering and annotation services to large technology and AI customers.
