MRVL — Marvell Technology, Inc.

Technology · Semiconductors

About Marvell Technology, Inc.

Marvell Technology, Inc., together with its subsidiaries, provides data infrastructure semiconductor solutions and spanning the data center core to network edge in the United States, Argentina, China, India, Israel, Japan, Singapore, South Korea, Taiwan, Vietnam, and internationally. The company develops and scales system-on-a-chip architectures, integrating analog, mixed-signal, and digital signal processing functionality.

The semiconductor industry

Semiconductors are the physical foundation of the AI build-out — the chips that train and run every model. It is a cyclical, capital-intensive industry split between fabless designers, who design chips and outsource production; foundries, who manufacture them; and integrated device makers. AI has become the dominant demand driver, concentrating spending on data-center accelerators, custom silicon, high-bandwidth memory and the networking that stitches them together. The sector is also unusually exposed to export controls, manufacturing concentration in Taiwan, and sharp boom-bust capex cycles. Marvell designs custom silicon and the optical and networking chips that move data inside and between AI data centers.
