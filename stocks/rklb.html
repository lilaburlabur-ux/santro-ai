RKLB — Rocket Lab Corporation

Industrials · Aerospace & Defense

About Rocket Lab Corporation

Rocket Lab Corporation, a space company, provides launch services and space systems solutions in the United States, Canada, Japan, and internationally. The company operates through launch services and space systems segments. The company provides launch services, spacecraft design services, spacecraft components, spacecraft manufacturing, optical systems, and other spacecraft and on-orbit management solutions and constellation management services, as well as designs and manufactures small and medium-class rockets and develops flight and ground software.

The commercial space industry

The commercial space industry spans rocket launch, satellite manufacturing, Earth-observation imaging and space-based communications. It is capital-intensive and still maturing, but launch costs have fallen sharply and demand is rising from defense, communications and data customers. The link to AI is mainly data and autonomy: space systems generate vast imagery and sensor streams that AI models analyze, and increasingly rely on onboard autonomy — making this an adjacent layer of the broader AI-and-data build-out rather than a pure AI play. Rocket Lab provides small-satellite launch and builds spacecraft and space systems, positioning it across both launch and manufacturing.
